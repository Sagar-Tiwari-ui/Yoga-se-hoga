About Yoga of Himalayas

Preserving Ancient Traditions, Embracing Modern Science

Yoga of Himalayas is a premier yoga institute dedicated to promoting the ancient science of yoga in its most authentic and comprehensive form. Nestled in the serene Himalayan foothills, our institute provides an ideal environment for learning, practicing, and experiencing the transformative power of yoga.

Established with the vision of making traditional yoga accessible to all, we offer a wide range of programs from beginner courses to advanced teacher training certifications. Our experienced faculty comprises renowned yoga masters, scholars, and practitioners who bring decades of expertise and dedication to every class.

We believe in the holistic approach to yoga, integrating asanas (postures), pranayama (breathing techniques), meditation, philosophy, and lifestyle practices to promote complete physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.
