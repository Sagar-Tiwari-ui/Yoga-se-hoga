Our Esteemed Faculty

Learn from experienced masters dedicated to preserving authentic yoga traditions

Home › About Us › Faculty

At Yoga of Himalayas, our faculty comprises highly qualified and experienced yoga masters who have dedicated their lives to the practice and teaching of authentic yoga. Each instructor brings a unique blend of traditional knowledge and modern teaching methodologies, ensuring that our students receive the highest quality yoga education.

Director & Lead Instructor

Director & Founder

C. S. Pandey

E-RYT 500, Director & Senior Yoga Master

Chandra Shekhar Pandey is a qualified Yoga Instructor and wellness professional with strong academic grounding in Yogic Science and hands-on experience in therapeutic and fitness-based yoga practices. He is currently serving as a Yoga Instructor at a Health and Wellness Center under the National Health Mission and is also affiliated as a Serving Secretary with Adi Advait Sansthan.

His approach to teaching combines traditional yogic practices with modern exercise science, anatomy, and rehabilitation techniques. With specialized training in functional training, weight loss, suspension training, and prehab/rehab conditioning, he focuses on safe, result-oriented yoga and fitness programs tailored for diverse age groups and physical needs.

Meet our team of dedicated yoga masters

Dr. Vikash Joshi

Senior Faculty – Science & Yogic Studies

Dr. Vikash Joshi holds a Ph.D. in Physics from Germany and brings a strong scientific and analytical perspective to yogic and wellness studies. With a deep interest in the intersection of modern physics, human consciousness, and ancient Indian knowledge systems, he contributes a rational, evidence-based approach to understanding mind–body practices.

Connection of yoga with Theoretical Physics Science & Consciousness Ancient Indian Knowledge Systems

Dr. Satya Prakash Joshi

Senior Faculty – Chemistry & Yogic Philosophy

Dr. Satya Prakash Joshi holds a Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of Helsinki and brings a strong scientific foundation to the study of meditation, philosophy, and holistic well-being. His academic background enables him to explore yogic and meditative practices through a rational, analytical, and research-oriented lens.

Connection of yoga with Physical & Theoretical Chemistry Science of Meditation Philosophy of Mind

Mr. Kailash Joshi

Faculty – Yogic Philosophy

Mr. Kailash Joshi is a dedicated teacher of Yogic Philosophy with a deep understanding of classical yogic texts and ethical frameworks. His teachings emphasize the philosophical foundations of yoga, including the concepts of yama and niyama, the eightfold path of Ashtanga Yoga, and the integration of yogic wisdom into daily life.

Yoga Sutras of Patanjali Ashtanga Yoga Philosophy Yama & Niyama
