Designed for Your Practice

Our campus is thoughtfully designed to create the perfect environment for yoga learning and practice. Located in the serene foothills of the Himalayas, our facilities combine traditional aesthetics with modern amenities.

Our Facilities

Main Yoga Halls

Three spacious, air-conditioned yoga halls with wooden floors and panoramic mountain views. Each hall is equipped with professional lighting, sound systems, and can accommodate up to 50 students.

  • Bamboo hardwood flooring for optimal practice
  • Natural ventilation with mountain breeze
  • Professional audio systems for guided sessions
  • Wall-to-wall mirrors and props storage
  • Temperature-controlled environment

Meditation Spaces

Dedicated meditation halls designed for peace and introspection. Traditional architecture creates a sacred atmosphere perfect for deep meditation practice.

  • Quiet, secluded location away from main buildings
  • Traditional Indian meditation cushions and props
  • Incense and natural aromatherapy
  • Acoustic design for chanting and mantras
  • Open-air meditation terrace

Residential Accommodation

Comfortable rooms with stunning Himalayan views. Choose from shared dormitories or private rooms, all maintained to high standards of cleanliness and comfort.

  • Single, double, and shared accommodation options
  • Attached bathrooms with hot water
  • Mountain-facing balconies
  • Daily housekeeping service
  • Eco-friendly amenities

Dining Facilities

Spacious dining hall serving nutritious sattvic vegetarian meals. Our kitchen uses organic ingredients sourced from local farms, prepared with love and yogic principles.

  • 100% vegetarian organic cuisine
  • Accommodates dietary restrictions and allergies
  • Fresh ingredients from local organic farms
  • Open-air seating with garden views
  • Purified drinking water stations

Yoga Library & Resource Center

Extensive collection of yoga texts, philosophy books, and research papers. Quiet study areas perfect for self-study and research.

  • Over 2,000 books on yoga and philosophy
  • Ancient Sanskrit texts and translations
  • Digital resources and video library
  • Reading rooms with comfortable seating
  • Research assistance available

Yoga Therapy Center

Specialized facility for therapeutic yoga sessions and consultations. Equipped with props, equipment, and private consultation rooms.

  • Private therapy consultation rooms
  • Specialized equipment for therapeutic yoga
  • Ayurvedic treatment facilities
  • Trained therapeutic yoga specialists
  • Personalized treatment plans
