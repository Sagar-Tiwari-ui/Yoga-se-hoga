Our Journey

Yoga of Himalayas was founded with a vision to preserve and share the authentic teachings of yoga in their purest form. What began as a small ashram in the foothills of Rishikesh has grown into one of India's most respected yoga institutions, touching the lives of thousands of students from around the world.

Our Timeline

The Beginning

C. S. Pandey establishes Yoga of Himalayas as a small ashram in Rishikesh, dedicated to traditional yoga teachings with just 5 students in the first batch.

2000
2005

International Recognition

First international students arrive from Europe and North America. The institute gains recognition from Yoga Alliance USA, enabling certified teacher training programs.

Campus Expansion

New dedicated campus opens with modern facilities including yoga halls, meditation spaces, library, and residential accommodation for 100+ students.

2010
2015

Yoga Therapy Center

Launch of specialized Yoga Therapy department with certified therapists. Introduction of therapeutic programs for various health conditions.

10,000+ Graduates

Milestone achievement of training over 10,000 certified yoga teachers from 50+ countries. Establishment of alumni network spanning the globe.

2018
2020

Digital Transformation

Launch of comprehensive online yoga platform during pandemic, making authentic teachings accessible globally through live-streamed classes and virtual teacher trainings.

Research & Innovation

Establishment of Yoga Research Center in collaboration with leading universities. Publication of research papers on therapeutic applications of yoga.

2023
2026

Continuing the Legacy

Today, Yoga of Himalayas stands as a beacon of authentic yoga education, continuing to inspire and transform lives while staying true to traditional teachings.

Our Legacy

Building a Global Yoga Community

Over the past two decades, we have remained committed to our founding principles while evolving to meet the needs of modern practitioners. Our alumni have gone on to establish yoga studios, wellness centers, and educational programs in over 60 countries.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to authenticity. We don't dilute or commercialize yoga teachings. Instead, we present them in their traditional form while making them accessible and relevant to contemporary life.

Core Values Since 2000:

  • Preserving authentic yogic traditions and lineage
  • Providing accessible, high-quality yoga education
  • Fostering a supportive, inclusive learning community
  • Integrating ancient wisdom with modern science
  • Promoting holistic wellbeing and conscious living
  • Serving humanity through yoga seva (service)
