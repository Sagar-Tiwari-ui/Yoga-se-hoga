Our Journey

Two decades of preserving ancient wisdom and transforming lives through authentic yoga education

From Humble Beginnings to Global Recognition

Founded in 2000 in the spiritual heart of Rishikesh, Yoga of Himalayas began with a simple vision: to preserve and share the authentic teachings of yoga with seekers from around the world. What started as a small ashram with 12 dedicated students has grown into a globally recognized institution, touching the lives of over 15,000 students from 60+ countries.

2000

The Beginning

Yoga of Himalayas was established in the sacred city of Rishikesh with a vision to preserve traditional yoga teachings. Started with a small ashram and 12 dedicated students who shared our passion for authentic yoga practice.

2003

Yoga Alliance Recognition

Received official accreditation from Yoga Alliance, USA, establishing our global credibility and allowing us to offer internationally recognized teacher training certifications to students worldwide.

2005

First International Retreat

Organized our first international yoga retreat, welcoming practitioners from 15 countries. This marked our expansion into global yoga education and cultural exchange programs.

2008

New Facility Expansion

Inaugurated our purpose-built yoga center with modern facilities while maintaining traditional architectural aesthetics. Capacity expanded to accommodate 100+ students simultaneously.

2012

Yoga Therapy Department

Launched specialized Yoga Therapy programs integrating ancient healing techniques with modern therapeutic approaches. Trained faculty in therapeutic applications of yoga for various health conditions.

2015

Online Learning Platform

Launched our digital learning platform, making authentic yoga education accessible to students who cannot travel to Rishikesh. Offering live-streamed classes and comprehensive online courses.

2018

Research Partnerships

Established collaborations with leading universities for yoga research, contributing to scientific validation of traditional practices and their benefits for modern health challenges.

2020

20th Anniversary

Celebrated two decades of service with special programs and the launch of scholarship initiatives to support dedicated students from underprivileged backgrounds pursuing yoga education.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Implemented comprehensive eco-friendly practices including solar power, rainwater harvesting, and organic gardens. Committed to being a carbon-neutral institution by 2025.

Building a Global Legacy

Over the past two decades, we have remained committed to our founding principles while evolving to meet the needs of modern practitioners. Our alumni have gone on to establish yoga studios, wellness centers, and educational programs in over 60 countries, spreading the light of authentic yoga teachings worldwide.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to authenticity. We don't dilute or commercialize yoga teachings. Instead, we present them in their traditional form while making them accessible and relevant to contemporary life. This balance between honoring ancient wisdom and embracing modern understanding is at the heart of everything we do.
