Teacher Training

200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training

4 Weeks Intensive or 3 Months Weekend

Yoga Alliance certified foundational teacher training program. Even if you don't plan to teach, this comprehensive course deepens your practice and understanding of yoga on all levels.

Certification

Yoga Alliance RYT 200 certification upon completion. Internationally recognized credential to teach worldwide

Course Content

Asanas, pranayama, meditation, anatomy, philosophy, teaching methodology, ethics, and practice teaching

Study Modes

Intensive residential (4 weeks) or weekend program (3 months) - both include same comprehensive curriculum

Program Modules

1
Yoga Techniques (100 hours)

Asana practice, pranayama, kriyas, meditation, chanting, and mantra practice with detailed alignment

2
Teaching Methodology (25 hours)

Teaching skills, class sequencing, demonstration, observation, assisting, instruction, communication

3
Anatomy & Physiology (20 hours)

Human anatomy, physiology of yoga practice, common injuries, modifications, and contraindications

4
Yoga Philosophy (30 hours)

Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita, ethics for teachers, yoga lifestyle, history and traditions of yoga

5
Practicum (10 hours)

Practice teaching, receiving feedback, observation, assisting classes, developing teaching voice

6
Electives (15 hours)

Ayurveda basics, adjustment techniques, yoga therapy introduction, business of yoga, specialized workshops
