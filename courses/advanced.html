Advanced Certification

300-Hour Advanced Teacher Training

5 Weeks Intensive or 4 Months Weekend

Prerequisites

Completion of 200-hour teacher training (RYT 200) or equivalent experience. Minimum 2 years of regular yoga practice and teaching experience preferred.

Yoga Alliance certified 300-hour advanced training that builds upon your foundational knowledge. Explore advanced asanas, subtle body anatomy, advanced philosophy, and develop your unique teaching voice. Upon completion, register as RYT 500 with Yoga Alliance.

Advanced Techniques

Master arm balances, inversions, backbends, and complex sequencing with proper alignment and modifications

Teaching Excellence

Refine teaching skills, voice projection, class management, business skills, and marketing strategies

Specialization Tracks

Choose focus areas: therapeutic yoga, prenatal, seniors, athletes, or meditation intensive

300-Hour Curriculum Breakdown

1
Advanced Asana Practice (75 hours)

Complex poses, arm balances, advanced inversions, deep backbends, peak pose sequencing, variations and modifications

2
Pranayama & Subtle Body (40 hours)

Advanced breathing techniques, bandhas, mudras, chakra system, nadis, koshas, energy body practices

3
Advanced Philosophy (50 hours)

Deep study of Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita, Hatha Yoga Pradipika, Upanishads, Sanskrit, yogic lifestyle

4
Teaching Methodology (60 hours)

Advanced sequencing, theme-based classes, hands-on assists, voice development, class management, business

5
Anatomy & Therapeutics (40 hours)

Advanced anatomy, injury prevention, therapeutic applications, common conditions, safe modifications

6
Practicum & Mentorship (35 hours)

Practice teaching, mentored sessions, peer feedback, personal practice development, case studies

Advanced Asana Mastery Program

3 Weeks Intensive

Prerequisites

Intermediate to advanced level practitioners with established practice. Ability to hold basic inversions and arm balances safely.

Intensive program focusing exclusively on advanced asana practice. Perfect for practitioners who want to master challenging poses, understand biomechanics, and break through practice plateaus with expert guidance.

Advanced Poses

Handstands, forearm stands, scorpion, flying crow, king pigeon, wheel variations, and more

Safe Progression

Systematic approach to advanced poses with proper prep, alignment, strength building, and injury prevention

Personal Attention

Maximum 12 students per batch ensures individualized feedback and hands-on adjustments

Weekly Focus Areas

1
Week 1: Inversions & Core

Headstand variations, forearm stands, handstand prep, core strengthening, shoulder stability, overcoming fear

2
Week 2: Arm Balances & Transitions

Crow family, eight angle, peacock, side crow, flying pigeon, smooth transitions, balance refinement

3
Week 3: Backbends & Integration

Deep backbends, wheel variations, king pigeon, kapotasana, integration sequences, sustainable practice
