Meet Your Therapist

Dr. C. S. Pandey

Senior Yoga Therapist, C-IAYT

Dr. Pandey brings over 4 years of experience in yoga therapy with specialized training in chronic pain management and stress-related disorders. He holds a M.A. in Yoga Therapy and is certified by the International Association of Yoga Therapists.

His compassionate, evidence-based approach combines traditional yogic wisdom with modern medical understanding to create effective healing protocols for each individual.
